College Program/(Credential Level) Graduation Rates

Graduation rate —the percentage of students who were reported to have exited the programs.

College Program/(Credential Level) Employment Rates

Employment rate of program completers – the percentage of program completers who earned positive reported earnings in the three months’ period when they completed the program or in the following six months. Completers are those who were reported to have exited the programs.

College Program/(Credential Level) Retention Rates

Employment retention rate – the percentage of program completers who earned positive reported earnings in the first three months’ period when they completed program and in the tenth to twelfth months after the first three months’ period when they completed program. Completers are those who were reported to have exited the programs.

College Program/(Credential Level) Median Annual Earnings

Average annual earnings —the median value of a specified group of completers’ total earnings from the first four consecutive earning quarters. An earning quarter is a quarter in which an employee is reported with positive earnings. A year has four quarters: the first quarter includes January through March; the second quarter includes April through June; the third quarter includes July through September, and the last quarter includes October through December. Completers are those who were reported to have exited the programs.

College Program/(Credential Level) Transfer Rates

• Transfer rate — the percentage of students who were reported to be transferred to another institution.
